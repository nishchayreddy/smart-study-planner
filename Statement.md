# Project Statement

## Problem

Many students face difficulty in managing multiple subjects, deadlines, and study priorities. They often waste time deciding what to study first and how to divide their time efficiently.

## Objective

The objective of this project is to build a simple and effective study planner that helps students organize their study schedule based on subject difficulty and deadlines.

## Approach

The system takes input such as subjects, number of days left, and difficulty level. Based on this, it calculates priority using a combination of urgency and difficulty. The planner then generates a day-wise schedule by distributing available study hours proportionally.

## Features

* Priority-based scheduling
* Day-wise study plan generation
* Interactive progress tracker using checkboxes
* Simple and user-friendly interface

## Outcome

The project successfully generates a structured study plan and allows users to track their progress. It helps in improving time management and productivity.
